WHAT ARE K-DRAMAS?

K-dramas (shorter term for korean dramas) are tv shows made in South Korea. When it comes to k-dramas, there are many different genres to choose from such as action, romantic comedy, etc.

REASONS TO START WATCHING K-DRAMAS

1. Length

When comparing American TV shows to K-dramas, one noticeable difference between the two is the length of each series. K-dramas tend to only last one season with about 16-30 episodes, while other tv shows such as Grey’s anatomy tend to run for many seasons. The length of k-dramas allows watchers to see the character development more effectively and end the show on a final note.

2. Culture

One of the great things about watching k-dramas is getting to learn more about Korean Culture. K-drama’s use many things such as clothing, food, and language to show different aspects of Korean culture.

3. Unique/Unexpected Storylines

When it comes to k-dramas, it is very hard to get bored because of the unique storylines each k-drama has to offer. The unique elements present in each k-drama will make you stay at the edge of your seat.
